## â“ FAQ

### **Q: What's the purpose of 4 random digits?**
A: To ensure uniqueness. Even if someone else chooses "pipulse", you get `pipulse-7293` and they get `pipulse-5142`.

### **Q: Can I change it after setting?**
A: Possibly, but it's best to choose wisely the first time.

### **Q: Does this affect my Vercel URL?**
A: No, they're completely separate. You still have `pipulse-five.vercel.app`.

### **Q: Can users use both URLs?**
A: Yes! Both work and lead to your app.

### **Q: What if "pipulse" is already taken?**
A: System will show error. Try an alternative like `pi-pulse` or `pipulse-app`.

### **Q: Is it case-sensitive?**
A: No, system converts to lowercase automatically.

### **Q: What about special characters?**
A: Only letters, numbers, and hyphens allowed. No underscores, spaces, or symbols.

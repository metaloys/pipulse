## â“ Common Questions

### **Q: Will the random digits change?**
A: No, they're assigned once and stay the same. Your PiNet URL is permanent.

### **Q: Can I use a different subdomain?**
A: Yes! See PINET_SUBDOMAIN_SETUP.md for alternatives.

### **Q: Do I need both Vercel URL and PiNet URL?**
A: Yes! They serve different purposes:
- **Vercel**: Direct access, sharing links
- **PiNet**: Access through Pi Network's app store/ecosystem

### **Q: What if my subdomain is taken?**
A: Pi Network will likely show an error. Try another name.

### **Q: What about uppercase letters?**
A: System will convert to lowercase (pipulse = PIPULSE â†’ pipulse)
